### Topic discussion facilitator (English)

I am a Postpartum Doula and Early Parenting Guide based in Amsterdam.
I believe that when you give birth to your baby, you are also giving birth to a new version of yourself.
A version that never existed before. This transformation is big! It's huge! and it's greatly undervalued and unseen in our culture.

As a discussion facilitator and Postpartum Doula I bring peace and joy to the first weeks of your transition to parenthood.
I answer your questions, connect you with local resources, work with you as a family/couple, and make sure you feel supported.

_To learn more about Danielle or book her privately, please visit [Mothers Transition](https://www.motherstransition.com/)_.
