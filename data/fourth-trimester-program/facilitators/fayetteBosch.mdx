### Topic discussion facilitator (Dutch)

I support pregnant women in moving from feeling overwhelmed to feeling in control and confident during pregnancy, birth, and the fourth trimester, so they can recover both physically and mentally and combine work and motherhood with confidence. I do this through the [Mama Thrive Program](https://mamathrive.nl/programma/), which includes a fourth trimester workshop, postpartum support at home, coaching in early motherhood, and guidance in the transition back to work.

I know what it’s like to go through the transition from woman to mother. What surprised me most was how little attention is given to what happens after birth. Everyone focuses on the baby checklist and the birth itself, while the real transformation actually begins afterward: recovering, adjusting to your new role, and finding your way back to work while you have fundamentally changed.

The transition from woman to mother is structurally underestimated, even though this phase largely determines how you feel physically and mentally in the years that follow. And that needs to change. That’s why I founded Mama Thrive.
