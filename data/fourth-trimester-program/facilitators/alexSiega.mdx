### Program admin & socials facilitator

I'm a technologist with years of experience running mission-based volunteer groups and nonprofits.
After moving to Amsterdam mid-pandemic and becoming a mom in 2023,
I saw firsthand how hard it is to build the postpartum support network: especially for expats navigating an unfamiliar culture with fewer resources.
I've combined my background in organizing with my passion for bringing people together to create a space where we can share the "full load" of parenthood.
Whether I’m streamlining program logistics or facilitating our social meetups, my goal is to make sure no one has to navigate newborn parenthood without the support they need: right when, where, and how they need it.
